## 📋 What is owly?

owly is a lightweight app that lets you join video calls quickly. It works well on phones and computers. The app focuses on giving you clear video quality. It also helps groups work together with tools that make collaboration easy.

## 🎥 How to Use owly

owly is designed to be easy. Here is how you can start video calls:

- **Create a Room:** Press the “Create Room” button to start a new video chat. You can invite others by sharing the room link.

- **Join a Room:** If you have a link from someone else, paste it in the “Join Room” box and press Enter.

- **Adjust Video:** Use the settings to turn your camera or microphone on or off. You can also change video quality if needed.

- **Collaboration Tools:** There are options to share your screen and chat with others while on a call. Use the buttons on the right side.

## 📝 Troubleshooting

If you run into problems, try these tips:

- Check your internet connection. A weak connection can cause poor video quality.
- Make sure your camera and microphone are not used by other apps.
- Restart the app if it freezes or does not respond.
- Close other programs that might slow down your computer.
- Check if Windows is up-to-date.
- Try restarting your computer if problems continue.

## 🔒 Privacy and Security

owly uses WebRTC, which sends data directly between users without storing it on servers. This means:

- Your video and audio stay private during calls.
- The app does not save or record your calls unless you choose to.
- You control who joins your room by sharing the link only with people you trust.
